I am broadly interested in open source, natural language processing, applying language models to code, adversarial AI, and turning research code into production.

Specifically, my work has entailed researching, training, testing, and deploying models in both TensorFlow and PyTorch for natural language processing, adversarial computer vision and text, and acoustics. I've also worked on simulation and tooling for reinforcement learning.

I enjoy making code pretty and automating as much of my job as possible.
